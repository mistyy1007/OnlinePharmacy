Frequently Asked Questuons

Couldn't find what you what? Feel free to contact us!

1. Can I place an order without creating an account?

Yes! We want to make everything easy for you. You can checkout as a guest if you want. However, registered customers can check order status and history anytime they want. First purchase of new customer will get 10% off!

2. Do I need a prescription to place an order including prescription medications?

Yes. Like your local pharmacy, UPMC Pharmacy requires a valid prescription written by a licensed physician, in order for us to dispense your prescription medications. All prescription medications are clearly marked by "RX". Without a valid prescription for such items, we will not be able to process and ship your order. Over-the-counter products do not require a prescription and are indicated by "OTC".

3. Do you ship oversea?

Yes, although UPMC Pharmacy is a local online pharmacy, we also ship worldwide. However, only US customers will get free shipment over $50 order, other purchases have various shipment fee, please call us to get detailed information.

4. How long will it take for my order to arrive?

You will see the approximate days that your order needs to arrive when you choose shipment method during checkout process. If you are our registereded customer, you can track your order under "Your Account".
